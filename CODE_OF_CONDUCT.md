# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

The Zabbix Enterprise Monitoring Guide is intended to provide a professional, respectful and technically focused environment for collaboration.

All participants are expected to contribute constructively, regardless of experience level, background or organizational affiliation.

---

## Expected Behaviour

Participants should:

- communicate respectfully;
- provide constructive technical feedback;
- focus discussions on improving the project;
- acknowledge differing professional opinions;
- avoid unnecessary conflict;
- support accurate and responsible documentation.

---

## Unacceptable Behaviour

The following behaviour is not accepted:

- harassment or discrimination;
- personal attacks;
- offensive or hostile language;
- deliberate misinformation;
- spam or unrelated promotional content;
- publishing private or sensitive information;
- disrupting technical discussions intentionally.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project-related spaces, including:

- GitHub Issues;
- Pull Requests;
- Discussions;
- documentation contributions;
- other communication related to the project.

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, remove or reject content that does not comply with these guidelines.

Repeated or serious violations may result in temporary or permanent restrictions from participating in the project.

---

## Reporting

Concerns regarding behaviour within the project may be reported through the contact channels listed on the SARABEL Informatika website.

Reports will be reviewed responsibly and handled with appropriate discretion.
